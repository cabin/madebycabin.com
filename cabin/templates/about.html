After a decade of leading design and technology efforts for teams at Etsy, GOOD, Blurb, and beyond, one blaring fact demanded attention. Nothing got done better or faster (or with the same dollop of fun) as the times when these two worked side by side. Partners

Bek Hodgson

Founder / Creative Director

Bek Hodgson is a designer and strategist who has worked to define and extend social good brands for over a decade. With experience spanning traditional print, digital design, marketing, and strategy, Bek has spent her career building tools and environments for communities to thrive.

Originally from Santa Barbara, California—and a bona fide gypsy at heart—Bek calls Zak home. Where her caravan goes only one man knows.

@uberbek Instagram Pinterest

Zak Johnson

Founder / Technologist

Zak Johnson has architected and developed software for over a decade. His passion for crafting experiences that are fun, easy to use, and reliable has defined his work. Zak's intimate familiarity with the entire web stack—from infrastructure through database, back end, and front end—enables him to build full-scale web applications.

Born in Brisbane, Australia and raised in the Pacific Northwest, Zak now calls Portland, Oregon home.

@zakj Instagram GitHub
